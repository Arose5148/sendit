About SendIt

Carmen, the driving force behind SendIt, has been living and breathing the dirt bike life in the Laurel Highlands for over 40 years. From suspension services to custom wheel setups for shredding the gnarliest tracks, he's got the passion and expertise to help you unleash your machine's full potential.

He's also the man when it comes to custom metal coatings. Got a hub that needs vapor blasting? Done. Want to Cerakote your engine parts or even your guns? Carmen's all over it.
